# 📊 Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior to understand **why customers churn** and how businesses can **improve retention**.

The analysis is based on a telecom dataset and simulates real-world business scenarios commonly faced by SaaS and subscription-based companies.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze **churn rate and retention trends**
- Identify **high-risk customer segments**
- Perform **cohort analysis**
- Understand **customer lifetime patterns**
- Provide **actionable business recommendations**

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib)
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
- Excel (Data understanding)

---

## 📁 Dataset
- Telco Customer Churn Dataset  
- Includes customer demographics, services, tenure, and churn status

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Churn Rate & Retention
- Calculated overall churn rate (~26%)
- Identified retention patterns across customers

### 2. Customer Cohorts
- Contract-based analysis (Month-to-month, yearly)
- Payment method analysis
- Internet service-based segmentation

### 3. Customer Lifetime Patterns
- Analyzed tenure to understand customer lifespan
- Identified early-stage churn behavior

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- **Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate (57%)**
- **Long-term contracts significantly reduce churn**
- **Early-stage customers (0–1 month) are most likely to churn**
- **Electronic check users show the highest churn rate**
- **Fiber optic users have higher churn compared to others**
- **Long-term customers are more loyal and stable**

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Offer discounts on **long-term contracts**
- Improve **onboarding experience** in first 3 months
- Encourage **auto-payment methods**
- Focus on **customer support for new users**
